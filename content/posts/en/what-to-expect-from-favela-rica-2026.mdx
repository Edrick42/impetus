June 26th and 27th. Mark your calendar. Favela + Rica 2026 will take place in Rio de Janeiro and Instituto Impetus is the official sponsor of this edition.

## Why this event matters

It's not just another event. It's one of very few spaces in Brazil where favela entrepreneurs, institutional investors and community leaders meet as equals — to do business, exchange mentorship and build partnerships that go beyond the two event dates.

## What's on the agenda

- **Pitches**: structured rounds where entrepreneurs present their businesses directly to investors
- **Panels**: conversations on access to credit, market expansion and measurable social impact
- **Targeted networking**: one-on-one meetings based on investment thesis alignment
- **Cultural programming**: because favela is also art, and art also moves the economy

## How to follow along

Even if you can't be there in person, you can follow on our Instagram and the photo gallery that will be published here on the site in real time during the event.
